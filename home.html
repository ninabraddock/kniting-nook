About the Matilda Inn:

724 Mulbury Lane
Vail, CO 81657

Located in the heart of downtown Vail, the Matilda Inn possesses all the charms of a vintage European ski lodge, while offering all of the modern conveniences one would expect with a stay in Vail. Enjoy all mountain views from one of the many pools/hot tubs, a glass of wine by the fire, or get to the slope early for the first track runs!! FREE: WiFi. Smiles too. *NO RESORT FEES!* Ranked #1 Lodge in Vail on tripadvisor.com
